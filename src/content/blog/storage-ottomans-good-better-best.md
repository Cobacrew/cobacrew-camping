This holds Amazon's Overall Pick badge with 4.5 stars across 2,000+ ratings and 1,000+ bought in the past month. It's a compact folding cube — 12.2 x 16.1 x 12.2 inches — that holds up to 286 lb and collapses flat when you don't need it, so it works in a dorm room or a small apartment without committing real floor space.
